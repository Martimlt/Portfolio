Master of Sciences in Business Analytics

The Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) at UCSD Rady School of Management is a cutting-edge program designed for the digitally competitive business landscape. It features a full-time, STEM-designated curriculum enhanced with AI and Large Language Models, covering all facets of business analytics. This program, which can be completed in just 11 months, prepares graduates to be at the forefront of business data science, ready for a market projected to grow 25% in the next five years. It's an ideal pathway for those aspiring to integrate AI with business strategy and data-driven decision-making.
