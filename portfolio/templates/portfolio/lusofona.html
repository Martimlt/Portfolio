Information Technology Management

The Bachelor's degree in Information Technology Management aims to fill an increasing need within organizations to bridge business areas (Marketing, Sales, Operations, etc.) with one of the main areas of strategic support, Information Systems. The languages of these areas are different, so only employees who can understand how companies are structured and positioned in their markets, combined with technological knowledge, can enhance a perfect Technology-Business alignment. This is the focus for those who pursue a Bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems.
